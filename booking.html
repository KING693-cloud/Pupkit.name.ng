Schedule A Visit

Meeting your future companion is the most important step in the adoption process.

Steps to Success

  • Complete the request form accurately.
  • Our admin team verifies facility availability.
  • You will receive a confirmation call or message.
  • Arrive 10 minutes early for your session.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring a guest?

Yes, we encourage bringing family members to ensure everyone is comfortable with the new pet.

What should I bring?

Please bring a valid ID and, if you have other pets, a photo of your current home setup.

Is there a fee?

No, the meet-and-greet session is free, though adoption fees apply upon finalizing the process.
